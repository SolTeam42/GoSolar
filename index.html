How to use solar

Once you install solar panels in your household you should understand how to optimally use solar energy. Since most of the solar energy is produced during midday hours, that’s ideal time to use appliances that consume a lot of energy – such as dishwashers, washing machines and water heaters. Of course you can use them in any time of the day if you have batteries that stored solar energy for later usage. On the other hand in the morning and in the evening when there is little or no solar energy produced and you will rely on energy stored in battery and it would be best to use lower power appliances such as desktops, laptops, tv or lights for book reading.
